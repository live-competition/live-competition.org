Do You Dare To Compete?

LIVE Competition is a platform for undergraduate students and industry professionals to connect and make long-lasting connections. Bringing together 120 students across Canada, our conference offers limitless opportunity for delegates to showcase their skills and talent to our partners in case rounds, presentations, networking and more.

Introducing our title sponsor

How it works
Preliminary Case

Released in December

A case weeks ahead of LIVE for your team to prepare and present on the day of. Use this time to research, prepare and present the best case for industry reps on the day of!

Day 1

January 9

Our 120 delegates arrive to face off in a day of challenging competition rounds and presentations. In the evening we host Corporate Connections, a networking event with over 35 industry representatives.
Day 2

January 10

The second day of LIVE presents more opportunites for delegates to showcase their talents in competition rounds and workshops. In the evening, LIVE hosts a gala to award our top 5 teams and to end off the competition, delegates get to celebrate at a social!

What to Expect

Competition Rounds

Delegates tackle challenging and innovative case rounds at LIVE in head to head competition. We provide multi-faceted rounds that are integrated in various streams of business for delegates to showcase their talent.

Corporate Connections

This networking event is the perfect opportunity for our delegates to meet our partners. This is where business professionals meet the future of business.

Awards Gala

What better way to end two days of challenging competition than with an exciting awards gala. Enjoy a night of food and fun as we celebrate the end of the competition by congratulating the teams that triumphed over their fierce competitors.

Key Dates

Registration

Registration period is open from November 11th to November 24th at 11:59PM, at a cost of $240 per delegate.

Application Process

Form your dream team of 4 and register! Don’t have a full team? Don’t worry, we can match you with other students!

You’ll get an acceptance email 2-3 days after the registration deadline and the preliminary case will be released soon afterwards.
