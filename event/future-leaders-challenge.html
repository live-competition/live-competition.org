Discover the Leader in You

Future Leaders Challenge (FLC) is our high school tier competition hosted at the University of Toronto Scarborough, designed to challenge students in a day of case rounds, panels, and networking.

Why Compete?

University Experience

FLC gives high school students a glimpse into what university is like. Experience what it's like to compete at the University of Toronto, one of Canada's top schools and complete challenging business cases that will give you a taste of what's to come.

Build Your Network

There's no better time to meet new people and begin building a network that is integral for your future. FLC provides delegates the opportunity to develop meaningful connections with upper year university students.

Win Big

Showcase your skills through our interactive and innovative cases for huge rewards. FLC is a chance to win monetary and non-monetary prizes by showcasing your talent. We also distribute swag bags so you won't leave empty-handed!
