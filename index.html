The Future Leaders Challenge (FLC) is an innovative business case competition designed to give high school students with a passion for business the opportunity to face the risk, challenges, and rewards of managing their own companies.

LIVE Competition is a two-day national multidisciplinary case competition that brings together elite talent for delegates showcase their academic and professional skills while gaining practical knowledge in a realistic and interactive business environment.
