Dear Delegates, Faculty Advisors, Sponsors, and Friends,

Welcome! It is with great pleasure that I introduce and invite you all to LIVE Competition. Before you ask, LIVE stands for Leading Innovative Visions to Execution - a name, a goal, and a brand all wrapped up in one. Entering our fourteenth year of operations, LIVE hosts the best and brightest undergraduate students from all over Canada.

The premier management case competition, LIVE's curriculum challenges students to apply classroom concepts in real world scenarios across a wide variety of business disciples. Students go head to head in both domestic and international management issues that will ultimately emulate the world they will find themselves in upon graduation. In order to help prepare them, we aim to foster an attitude of innovation, entrepreneurship, and corporate social responsibility, while at the same time providing a platform to showcase to sponsors where their next pool of talented employees lies.

Now that you have a better idea of what LIVE is, I also get to introduce you to who LIVE is. We are a team of students drawn from all of UTSC's different faculties and departments, each of us committed to seeing this mission through and giving each of you the best LIVE you've ever seen. This team is filled with the biggest dreamers I never would have met in high school and I am honored to get a chance to work with them.

That said, we've got big things in store for the year, so I hope to see you out at LIVE 2019/2020 this January - as a competitor, a speaker, a sponsor, or any other part of our LIVE family.

Can't wait to meet you all!

Best regards,

Iram and Nicole
